About Me

I am a data management engineer and economic policy enthusiat working on my PhD in Educational Technology at the University of Florida, FL.

I am a 2021 Holmes Scholar at UF College of Education and passionate about the wholistic approach to learning, travel, photography and collaborating with people.

My research interest is the adopting analytical and data mining tools to draw insights from various learning management systems. I am originally from Ghana - West-Africa and I have worked in various countries including South Africa, Kenya, UAE, and England.
