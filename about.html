About Me

I am a data scientist and economic policy analyst working on my PhD in Educational Technology at the University of Florida, FL.

I am passionate about the wholistic approach to learning, travel, photography and collaborating with people.

My research interest is the adopting analytical and data mining tools to draw insights from various learning management systems.

I am originally from Ghana - West-Africa and I have worked in various countries including South Africa, Kenya, UAE, and England.
