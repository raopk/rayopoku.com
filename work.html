Final ID Project

At the end of the instructional design class, students are required to complete a final project. For this project, we were tasked to identify any problem that needed an instructional solution. To identify a problem, one must conduct a needs assessment. The idea here is to design a solution for a current felt need. From my needs assessment, I designed a module to introduce students to learning disabilities.

The needs assessment for this project revealed that students complete their first terms without any exposure to issues on learning disabilities. An instructional module to introduce students was designed in response to the results from the needs assessment.

At the end of the lesson, the expectation was for learners to become aware of the various learning disabilities. Upon completing the module, students will discuss the historical events that led to the passing of the Americans with Disabilities Act. Activities in the module includes an assessment quiz, an interactive video in PlayPosit, a discussion on Perusall and an essay assignment.
