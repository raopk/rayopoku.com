Design

.

For this assignment you will need to design an educational technology that will address an important problem of practice within the broad area of learning and teaching with technology. You will work on this project as a team with one or two peers and you will present your design using a website. Your technology may focus, for example, on early childhood education and learning to read via a mobile application that you design, a technology for reviewing complex plant taxonomies and phylogenies for exams in undergraduate botany courses, a technology for high schoolers to create and share videos on difficult algebra concepts etc. Note that in this class you will be designing a technology, not developing it. This project will consist of the following components: a) research and modeling, c) design requirements, and d) design framework and website.
